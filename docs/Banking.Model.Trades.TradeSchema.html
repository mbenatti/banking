    banking vdev Banking.Model.Trades.TradeSchema View Source

    Represent a Trade

    The trade is each Trade event, like Deposit, Withdrawal, Transfer received and issued and is backed on Banking.Model.Balances.EventSchema.t/0.

    The fields type, balance, quantity are virtual's field, necessary to build the reports, loaded from balance_event
